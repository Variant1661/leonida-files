Tips & tricks

Day-one survival playbook

Money and progression

Do not blow early cash on style-only purchases. First prioritise safehouses, reliable vehicles, weapons/tools, armour and mission unlocks. This is based on GTA V logic and should be updated once GTA VI’s economy is confirmed.

Police and heat management

Learn bridges, alleys, tunnels, car parks and water escape routes. GTA games reward route knowledge more than raw speed.

Vehicles

Keep one fast road car, one off-road vehicle, one bike and one boat/air option when available. Build your garage around mission flexibility.

Exploration

Before rushing the story, mark shops, garages, docks, weapon shops, hospitals, police stations and shortcuts. These become your survival map.
